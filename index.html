02 · The Architecture

Most trading bots run one strategy. SignalDelta runs two — at the same time, on the same market.

A baseline AI system runs alongside a second, independent system on the same trade infrastructure. The gap between their returns — once Lane 2 activates and is measured across hundreds of trades — is empirical evidence the second layer adds alpha.

LANE 1 — BASELINE · LIVE
A research-backed signal stack already profitable on its own.

A published-research foundation: a multi-indicator AI signal stack with a peer-reviewed 60.63% win rate. Industry-grade. The engine evaluates the market every 60 seconds and tracks up to 126 data points per cycle across crypto, stocks, news, market context, capital flow, and engine output.

Every trade carries 34 properties — every indicator reading, market condition, news event, stop management decision, and outcome metric. News is AI-classified twice daily into seven categories with impact ratings. Sharpe ratios are computed three ways. Returns are time-weighted and adjusted for capital flows.

Every Sunday, the system reviews the week's trades and refines its own rule set. The audit trail is complete.

Every trade, every rule, every config change is a permanent record — the system cross-references the full history to find patterns no human would spot.
